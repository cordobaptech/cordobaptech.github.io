Ecocolumn

Ecocolumn was another group assignment were we had to work together to take care of plants and fish. Before any of that we had to research on how ecocolumns worked. Then we had to use to bottle of soda to create an ecocolumn. During the process we had to figure out a way to connect all part of our ecocolumn. Then we had to plant and grow our plants. After that we checked our water to see if our fish could survive. Once that was all figured out we could add our fish. Everyday we had to water our plants , feed the fish and write down everything we did and our measurements. It was a bit stressful but our fish lived for a long time. It eventually died but at least we did everything we could to make it live as long as it could.
